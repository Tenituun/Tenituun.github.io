IDM, also known as "Intelligent dance music" is a style of electronic dance music created in the early 1990s. Originating from a combination of rave music and techno, breakbeat and ambient, IDM has a broad range of style and many subgenres. Notable artists in this genre are: Aphex Twin, Squarepusher and Venetian Snares.
